Murder Mystery Modules

As a founding member and former president of Brown University's Roleplaying and Narrativists Society, I write modules for Murder Mystery dinner parties. Each module is intended to take an hour to an hour and a half, and are based on the design principle of giving players creative freedom to interpret characters as they so desire. There are no scripted lines, but each character is given hints and abilities that bring them closer to the truth. Each module has a set of spoiler free instructions for the host, so they can play along if they wish.

You can find a selection of modules below. I am slowly moving the archives to a pdf format. Around 4 months a new module will be uploaded.
